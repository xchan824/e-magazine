Version 7.31c

Released on May 4th, 2022

Neutral Creeps

Harpy Scout
Take Off max mana cost per second from 2.5% to 4%
Vision reduced from 1800/1800 to 1200/800
Dark Troll Summoner
Cooldown increased from 18 to 20
Duration decreased from 40 to 35
Skeleton Warrior
Movement/Attack Speed slow against melee heroes rescaled from 25/25% to 20/30%
Movement/Attack Speed slow against ranged heroes rescaled from 50/50% to 40/60%

Heroes

Arc Warden
Base damage increased from 47-57 to 51-57
Axe
Base Health regeneration reduced from 2.75 to 2.5
Bane
Fiend's Grip Max Channel time reduced from 6 to 5/5.5/6s
Bloodseeker
Blood Mist self damage is now magical instead of Pure
Blood Mist damage to enemies increased from 5% to 7%
Blood Mist cooldown reduced from 4s to 3s
Broodmother
Spin Web mana cost reduced from 50 to 40
Chaos Knight
Chaos Bolt shard bonus range decreased from 300 to 150
Chaos Strike no longer applies Bonus damage to Creep Heroes and Roshan.
Chen
Base Movement Speed increased from 300 to 305
Hand of God cooldown decreased from 160/140/120 to 150/130/110
